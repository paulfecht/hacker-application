Our journeys in Nepal are specifically designed by, Kumar, a native of Nepal who resides in Colorado. Our adventures include some of the most spectacular scenery the world has to offer as well as experiencing trekking at its very best.

Enjoy the exotic food, traditional Nepali music, dancing and culture of the people in Nepal. Hiking and Kathmandu tours are provided by our associates and family in Nepal. Our associate trekking company in Kathmandu, Maya Devi Trekking and Company, will provide you with excellent guides and porters experienced in providing enjoyable and safe treks in the Himalayas.

If you are looking for a little more adventure we can arrange whitewater rafting or an elephant back jungle safari trip searching for rhinos and bengal tigers in the Royal Chitwan National Park.. Journeys can cater to all levels of adventures and personalize them to your wishes and needs.

Journeys specializes in guiding trekking trips throughout Nepal, including the the popular regions of Everest Base Camp, Annapurna Base Camp, Annapurna Circuit and the Langtang region.
